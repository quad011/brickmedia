(DR)TV Advertising Services


At Brick Media we create and produce advertising TV formats for international clients with a focus on direct response advertising, to get branded messages directly in front of the target audience. DRTV provides immediate customer feedback on campaigns and is ideal for introducing products/services to a large number of viewers.

TV marketing

Our TV marketing specialists will discuss your demand and develop a tailored format to get you the best results for your campaign. We produce in-house, minimizing costs while maximizing efficiency. The Brick Media team brings in years of TV production experience and knows, which content is trendy within your (potential) customer base.

We will advice you on every step of the production of your very own TV campaign. Our branded DRTV campaigns produce live lead generation with creative advertising and media buying that resonates directly with the consumer. We create and scale the direct response campaigns for optimal exposure for direct to consumer TV marketing. Whether working with your existing creative or scripting or directing & producing the advertising - we know how to buy media and monitor campaigns to directly reach the targeted audience.

NMC

NMC provides media tracking software which allows advertisers immediate confirmation on their campaigns performance – having access to the performance matrix gives confirmation. We capture the caller's information for your market research or future marketing initiatives.

With our DRTV solution we provide instant tracking and quantifiable lead generation as well as cost effectiveness. We calculate the efficiency of each commercial with an actual cost per lead. By doing so we know which media will perform well for your brand and which won’t, eliminating costs resulting in a more efficient media buy.

Performance

Regardless of how we work together, be it paid media or pay for performance, you can be confident that your media dollars are being spent as efficiently and effectively as possible. Because at Brick Media our focus is on performance based TV media campaigns that yield the highest ROI for you.
